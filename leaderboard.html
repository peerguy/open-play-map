Active credits Monthly drawing entries

Active credits keep stacking as you contribute and carry forward unless you win a monthly drawing. If you win, your active credits reset after that drawing.

Lifetime credits All-time contribution score

Lifetime credits never reset. They show your long-term impact and may unlock extra rewards for high earners in the future.

Monthly prize Free Scoop paddle

Each monthly drawing selects one active-credit entry at random. The winning player gets a free Scoop Pickleball paddle.

Rewards are in beta and subject to official terms before public launch. Credits have no cash value, are not transferable, and do not guarantee a prize. No purchase necessary. Eligibility, prize details, odds, winner confirmation, and drawing timing will be finalized before any prize is awarded.
