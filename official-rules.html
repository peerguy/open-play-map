Official Rules

Open Play Map Monthly Scoop Pickleball Drawing

Last updated: June 16, 2026. First drawing: August 1, 2026 at 5:00 PM Eastern Time.

No purchase necessary

NO PURCHASE OR PAYMENT OF ANY KIND IS NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. A purchase will not improve your chances of winning. The drawing is void where prohibited or restricted by law.

Sponsor

The sponsor is Scoop Pickleball, operator of Open Play Map. Questions about the drawing can be sent to scooppickleball@gmail.com.

Eligibility

The drawing is open to legal residents of the 50 United States and District of Columbia who are at least 18 years old at the time of entry and have a valid Open Play Map account in good standing. Employees, officers, contractors, and immediate family or household members of the Sponsor are not eligible. One account per person. Automated entries, duplicate accounts, fake accounts, spam, and attempts to manipulate credits or entries are prohibited.

Drawing schedule

Drawings are scheduled for the first day of each month at 5:00 PM Eastern Time, beginning August 1, 2026. Each drawing uses eligible active credits recorded at the time the drawing is run. If technical, operational, or legal issues delay a drawing, the Sponsor may run it as soon as reasonably practical after the scheduled time.

How to enter

Creating a free Open Play Map account automatically gives the entrant one active drawing entry. No contribution, purchase, review, photo, or location submission is required to receive this baseline free entry.

Entrants can earn additional active entries by making approved Open Play Map contributions. Current contribution credit values are shown on the Leaderboard and About pages. Credits tied to removed, rejected, duplicate, fake, spam, or otherwise invalid contributions may be removed.

Active credits carry forward from month to month unless the entrant is confirmed as a monthly drawing winner. After a confirmed win, the winner's active credits reset back to the baseline one active entry. Lifetime credits do not reset and are used only to show long-term contribution history.

Prize

Each monthly winner may choose either one paddle available for sale on www.scooppickleball.com, with standard U.S. shipping included, or up to $100 worth of gear from www.scooppickleball.com, with standard U.S. shipping included. Approximate retail value is up to $100 plus standard U.S. shipping. Prize availability may vary. No cash alternative is offered. The prize is not transferable. Sponsor may substitute a prize of equal or greater value if necessary.

Winner selection and odds

One winner is selected at random from all eligible active credits for the drawing. Each active credit equals one entry. Odds of winning depend on the total number of eligible active credits at the time of the drawing.

Winner notification and redraws

The potential winner will be contacted using the email address associated with their Open Play Map account. The potential winner has 14 calendar days from notification to respond and claim the prize. If the potential winner does not respond within that period, is found ineligible, declines the prize, cannot be contacted, or violates these rules, the Sponsor may disqualify that person and select an alternate winner by redraw from the same original drawing entry pool.

Taxes and expenses

Winners are responsible for any taxes, fees, or expenses not expressly included in these rules. Standard U.S. shipping for the selected prize is included.

Publicity and privacy

By accepting a prize, the winner permits Sponsor to display their Open Play Map username, prize, drawing month, and winning active credit count on Open Play Map and related Scoop Pickleball channels, unless prohibited by law. Personal information is handled according to the Privacy Policy.

General conditions

Sponsor may cancel, suspend, or modify a drawing if fraud, technical failures, abuse, legal restrictions, or other factors impair the integrity or operation of the drawing. Sponsor's decisions are final on all matters related to eligibility, entries, credits, winner selection, and prize fulfillment.

Release

By participating, entrants agree to release Sponsor and related parties from claims arising out of participation in the drawing or acceptance, use, misuse, or delivery of a prize, except where prohibited by law.
