# Open Play Map — Product Brief

## Purpose

Open Play Map is a searchable US directory of pickleball open play locations. It is designed for players who want to find public courts, know when open play happens, understand the likely skill level, and see what the location looks like before going.

## Core User Stories

- As a player, I want to search by city, state, park name, or ZIP area so I can find nearby open play.
- As a traveler, I want to scan a map for open play near where I’ll be staying.
- As a newer player, I want to know if the play is beginner-friendly before showing up.
- As a competitive player, I want to find intermediate/advanced open play.
- As a brand/operator, I want structured data that can later support submissions, rankings, events, and local guides.

## MVP Features

- Interactive map of court locations
- Searchable list
- Filters:
  - Free/public vs paid/private
  - Skill level
  - Indoor/outdoor
- Park detail cards
- Open-play hours section
- Photo section
- “Last verified” field

## Future Features

- User-submitted court updates
- Photo uploads
- Admin moderation dashboard
- Claim/manage location pages
- Ratings for open play quality
- Paddle demo/event tie-ins for Scoop Pickleball
- “Best open play near me” guides
- Mobile-first PWA
